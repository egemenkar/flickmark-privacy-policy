Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

FlickMark is designed with privacy in mind. We believe in minimizing data collection and protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Don't Collect

FlickMark does not collect, store, or process any personal information such as:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Your location data
  • Your photos or camera roll
  • Your personal files or documents
  • Your browsing history or usage patterns

Information We Do Collect

FlickMark only collects minimal technical information necessary for the app to function:

  • Device Information: Basic device type and operating system version for app compatibility
  • Subscription Status: Through our payment processor RevenueCat to manage your subscription
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous technical data to improve app stability

Third-Party Services

FlickMark uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription management and payment processing. RevenueCat may collect subscription-related data as outlined in their privacy policy.
  • OMDb API: For movie identification. We only send movie titles or images for identification purposes.
  • Google Gemini: For content analysis. Image data is processed but not stored.

Data Storage

  • All your movie lists and preferences are stored locally on your device only
  • We do not use any cloud storage services
  • Your data remains under your complete control
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all your data

How We Use Information

The minimal information we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the FlickMark service
  • Process subscription payments through RevenueCat
  • Improve app performance and fix technical issues
  • Ensure app compatibility with your device

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties, except:

  • RevenueCat for subscription processing
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With your explicit consent

Children's Privacy

FlickMark does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. If you are under 13, please do not use this app or provide any information.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's minimal data to manage. However, you have the right to:

  • Request information about any data we might have
  • Request deletion of any data (though we have very little)
  • Contact us with privacy-related questions

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
